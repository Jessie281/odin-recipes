Lamb Curry

What is it:

Lamb curry is a delicious and aromatic dish that combines tender pieces of lamb with a rich and flavorful curry sauce. It's a popular dish in many cultures and can be served with rice or bread.


Ingredients:

  • 500 g lamb, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Directions:

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the lamb pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, and coriander. Cook for 30 seconds until aromatic.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and chicken broth. Stir to combine.
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the lamb is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired, and serve with rice or bread.
