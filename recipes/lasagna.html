Lasagna, a classic!

What is it:

Lasagna is a type of pasta, one of the most popular dishes in Italy. It is made of sheets of pasta layered with meat, vegetables, and cheese.


Ingredients:

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 500 g ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Red wine (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • Seasonings of choice (e.g., oregano, thyme, onion powder, chili powder, italian seasoning)

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets according to the package instructions.
  3. In a large pan, heat the olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  5. Add bell pepper and cook until softened.
  6. Stir in the tomato sauce and tomato paste, and season with salt and pepper, and any desired seasonings.
  7. Simmer the sauce for 10 minutes.
  8. Spread a thin layer of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  9. Layer the lasagna sheets, meat sauce, and cheeses alternately.
  10. Top with a final layer of cheese.
  11. Bake for 30-35 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
