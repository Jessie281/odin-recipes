Coconut Lentil Bean Curry

What is it:

Coco lentil bean curry is a hearty and nutritious dish that combines the protein-rich lentils and beans with a creamy coconut milk base and aromatic spices. It's a great option for a vegetarian meal that's both filling and flavorful.


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of canned red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 cup of canned black beans, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 cup of vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Directions:

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the lentils and beans. Cook for a few minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, and ginger. Cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, and coriander. Cook for 30 seconds until aromatic.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Stir to combine.
  6. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the lentils are tender and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired, and serve with rice or bread.
